Longhouse has been deployed!

API Server:
  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "longhouse.fullname" . }}-api {{ .Values.service.api.httpPort }}:{{ .Values.service.api.httpPort }}

TCP/CSIL Server:
  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "longhouse.fullname" . }}-api {{ .Values.service.api.tcp.port }}:{{ .Values.service.api.tcp.port }}

Web UI:
  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "longhouse.fullname" . }}-web {{ .Values.service.web.port }}:{{ .Values.service.web.port }}

Then open http://localhost:{{ .Values.service.web.port }}/app/ in your browser.
